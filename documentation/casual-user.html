  • As a frequent user of this app, I want to get information on how to grow my plants, but also see my progress of gardening through pictures.

  • Julie gets home from work to see her snake plant hasn't had much progress from her last picture taken a week ago. She is feeling bummed and would like to know more information on how to have more progress within the upcoming week.
  • Precondition: Julie logs into her account on the app and compares her snake plant from the picture she has logged in on the app. She sees her snake plant hasn't made much progress and wants to look up tips for help.
  • Post condition: Julie logs her picture after following the website's instructions for growing her plant bigger and stronger. She will then wait a week to compare any progress.
